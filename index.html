About Beverly

Beverly is a vibrant city located along the North Shore of Massachusetts, offering a rich blend of history, arts, and natural beauty. Known as the "Birthplace of the American Navy," Beverly is home to miles of scenic coastline, thriving cultural institutions, and a welcoming community.

Our History

Founded in 1626, Beverly boasts a deep historical heritage. During the American Revolution, it played a pivotal role as the launching point for the first naval fleet of the United States. Its legacy is preserved in historic landmarks, such as the Balch House, one of the oldest wood-frame houses in America, and the Cabot Street Cinema Theatre, the oldest operating community theatre in the country.
